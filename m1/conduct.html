Code of Conduct

Based on Contributor Covenant — be respectful, constructive, and inclusive.

Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

Expected behavior

  • Use welcoming and inclusive language
  • Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
  • Accept constructive criticism gracefully
  • Focus on what is best for the community

Enforcement

Report issues via Contact. Maintainers may remove content or ban participants who violate this code.
