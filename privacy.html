Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Overview

OceanData4AI Community ("we", "us") operates oceandata4ai.community. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under GDPR and similar regulations.

Data we collect

  • Website analytics — Google Analytics 4 collects anonymized usage data (pages visited, referrer, device type).
  • Contact & Fellow forms — Name, email, and message content you voluntarily submit.
  • Third-party platforms — Discord, GitHub, and Medium have their own privacy policies when you use those services.

How we use data

We use collected data to operate the community, respond to inquiries, improve content, and measure site performance. We do not sell personal data.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies (GA4) with consent where required. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

Data retention

Form submissions are retained for up to 24 months unless you request deletion. Analytics data follows Google's default retention settings.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at contact. EU residents may lodge a complaint with their local supervisory authority.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be noted on this page with an updated date.
