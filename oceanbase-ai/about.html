About the OceanBase Community

A global open community for people who deploy, operate, and build on OceanBase and its ecosystem — vendor-neutral in spirit, engineering-first in practice.

Our mission

We help practitioners share production experience: migrations from MySQL, HTAP workloads, HA and backup, multi-tenant design, and AI-native data layers with seekdb and PowerMem. Questions belong in Ask OUG; long-form guides live on Medium and official docs.

Relationship with oceanbase.com

en.oceanbase.com is the official product site (pricing, sales, product blog). oceanbase.ai is the community hub — Q&A, events, and ecosystem landing pages. We link to each other; we don’t duplicate product marketing here.

What belongs here

Production-first Real deployment and ops notes — not marketing brochures.
Honest technical discourse Benchmarks, trade-offs, and postmortems welcome.
Ecosystem-inclusive OceanBase core plus seekdb, PowerMem, and community tools.
Respectful discourse Debate the tech, not the people. See our Code of Conduct.
